Error: Error: "Failed To Login: InvalidSession"

Solution: 1. Check if the Minecraft servers are down. You can check the status of the Minecraft servers on the official Minecraft Server Status page.
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies. Invalid sessions are often caused by corrupted or outdated cookies.
3. Restart your Minecraft launcher, making sure to fully close the application before restarting.
4. Verify your account credentials. Your account might have been compromised, or your login details might have been changed.
