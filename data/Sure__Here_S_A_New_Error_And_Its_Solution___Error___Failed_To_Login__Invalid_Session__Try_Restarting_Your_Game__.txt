Error: "Failed To Login: Invalid Session (Try Restarting Your Game)"

Solution: 1. Close Minecraft and reopen it. This will sometimes fix the issue and allow you to log back in.
2. Reset your internet connection. Disconnect from the internet and then reconnect to see if this clears up the issue.
3. Restart your computer. This will clear out any caches or temporary files that may be interfering with Minecraft's ability to connect to servers
