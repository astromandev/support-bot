Error: One Common Minecraft Error Is "Failed To Login: Invalid Session (Try Restarting Your Game)".

Step-By-Step

Solution: 1. Close your Minecraft launcher and any other instances of Minecraft that may be running.
2. Open your internet browser and log out of any Minecraft accounts that you are currently logged into.
3. Clear the browser cache and cookies.
4. Log back in to your Minecraft account on the official Minecraft website.
5. Launch the Minecraft launcher and log in with your username and password
