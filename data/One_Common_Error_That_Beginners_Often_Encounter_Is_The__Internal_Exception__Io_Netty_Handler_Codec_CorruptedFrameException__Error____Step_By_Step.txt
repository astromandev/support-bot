Error: One Common Error That Beginners Often Encounter Is The "Internal Exception: Io.Netty.Handler.Codec.CorruptedFrameException" Error. 

Step-By-Step

Solution: 1. Check your internet connection - this error is often caused by a weak or unstable internet connection.

2. Try restarting your modem and router - This can help refresh your connection and fix any errors.

3. Clear your Minecraft's cache - Go to the Minecraft launcher and click on the "Launch Options" tab. Under the version you are using
